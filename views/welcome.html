Flashward

Let your browser make flashcards.

Flashward helps you learn new foreign words in an integrated way. It automatically creates flashcards as you look up words in online dictionaries. You can review them repeatedly with properly scheduled intervals for memorization. (demo)

How to Use

1. Install Chrome extension.

2. Look up words in online dictionaries.

3. Review words on this website.

Chrome is only supported currently
